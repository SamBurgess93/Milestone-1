About Me

Growing up I always struggled with my weight making my way through school and college. I always wondered why did I look different. When I looked in the mirror I wasnt happy with the person looking back at me. My self confidence was at a real low and I wasn’t happy with the way I looked and I decided to do something about it. I started training and I soon realised it was my passion in life and wanted to help others on their journey too. The hardset step is the first one!
